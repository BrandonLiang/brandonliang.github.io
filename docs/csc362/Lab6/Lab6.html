Force-Directed Graph: Davidson College Academic Programs and Courses
Features:
1.Hover on nodes to View Course/MajorMinor info and Zoom in with D3 Fisheye plugin (Visual effect is better when the graph stablizes/nodes stop moving)
2.Drag nodes to new location for easier readability
3.Click Legend switches "Off" to make its clusters (all nodes of that Major or Minor) invisible/semi-transparent. Click "On" to make then visible again. Click "All Off" or "All On" to make all nodes and links off or on.
4.Click on any node to make its clusters (all nodes with the same Major or Minor) invisible/semi-transparent. Click again to make them visible again. The legend switches are automatically updated with status of every cluster.
